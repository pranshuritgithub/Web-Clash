Frequently asked questions

Yes, website builders and designers typically make sure that the websites they create are mobile-friendly or responsive. This means that the website will automatically adjust its layout and content to fit the screen size of the device it's being viewed on, ensuring that it's accessible and easy to use for all users, regardless of their device.

The time it takes to build a website varies depending on its complexity. A simple website can take a few days to a week, while a complex one can take several weeks or months. The timeline will be discussed and agreed upon during the initial consultation.

The cost of building a website depends on its complexity, number of pages, and required features. A simple website can cost a few hundred dollars, while a more complex website can cost several thousand dollars. The best way to get an accurate cost estimate is to discuss your needs with a website builder or designer and obtain a detailed quote.

The type of support offered after a website launch varies among website builders. Some offer ongoing maintenance for a fee, while others provide limited or no support. It's important to clarify the type of support included in the development package. Typically, builders provide some level of support, such as bug fixes and technical issue resolutions, and may also provide training on using the website.

Website builders and designers typically implement various security measures such as SSL certificates, firewalls, and regular software updates to ensure website security. They also follow best practices for password protection and data backup. Discussing security measures with your builder or designer is important to ensure a secure website.
